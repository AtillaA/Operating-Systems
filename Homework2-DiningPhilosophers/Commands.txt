--------------------------------------------------

Transfer a file from local machine to flow server:

	pscp -r code.cpp username@flow.sabanciuniv.edu:/home/username

--------------------------------------------------

g++ compilation:

	g++ -o code.out code.cpp

--------------------------------------------------

Compiling pthread programs:

	g++ -o code.out code.cpp –lpthread

--------------------------------------------------

Executing a compiled program:

	./code.out

--------------------------------------------------
